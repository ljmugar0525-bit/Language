Kindergarten Language

( 3rd Grading 1st Periodical Exam )


Part 1: Using Articles "A" and "An"
Instructions: Listen to the beginning sound. Choose "an" if the word starts with a vowel (A, E, I, O, U). Choose "a" for consonants.
Part 2: Identifying Letters
Instructions: Identify if the letter is a Vowel or a Consonant.
Part 3: Is vs. Are
Instructions: Use "is" for one (singular) and "are" for more than one (plural).
